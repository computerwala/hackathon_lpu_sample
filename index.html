About Us

The Urban Issue Reporter allows citizens to report and track urban issues in real-time. Help improve your city by sharing problems that need attention.

Urban issues such as potholes, streetlight failures, and garbage disposal problems can significantly impact city life. Our platform allows citizens to report these issues and monitor their resolution.

Real-Time Urban Issue Data

Live reports provide an overview of ongoing urban problems and their resolutions.

Impact of Urban Issues

Urban problems not only cause inconvenience but also impact the environment, economy, and public safety. Timely resolution of reported issues can lead to a better and more efficient city.
